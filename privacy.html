MyDailyScribe Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2025

Introduction

Thank you for choosing MyDailyScribe ("the Extension"), a browser extension designed to help users build a writing habit through daily writing prompts. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Summary

MyDailyScribe values your privacy and collects no personal information whatsoever. The Extension operates entirely locally on your device, requires no account creation, and does not track your usage or writing content.

Information We Do Not Collect

MyDailyScribe does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Your writing content
  • Browsing history
  • IP addresses
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics
  • Cookies or local storage data

How MyDailyScribe Works

  1. Local Operation: The Extension operates exclusively on your local device.
  2. No Authentication: No sign-up or authentication is required.
  3. No Server Communication: The Extension does not communicate with any external servers.
  4. No Data Storage: Your writing is not saved by the Extension and exists only in your browser until you close the Extension tab.

Browser Permissions

MyDailyScribe requires minimal permissions:

  • New Tab Permission: To open a writing prompt in a new tab.
  • Storage Permission: For storing your timer preferences locally on your device (this data never leaves your browser).

Third-Party Services

MyDailyScribe does not integrate with or utilize any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify users via the Extension's update notes.
