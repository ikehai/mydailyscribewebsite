Frequently Asked Questions

Is MyDailyScribe free to use?
Yes, MyDailyScribe is completely free with no hidden fees or premium features.
Do I need to create an account?
No, MyDailyScribe doesn't require any account creation or sign-up. Just install and start writing!
Can I save my writing?
Yes, you can copy your text to save it elsewhere. MyDailyScribe doesn't store your writing on any servers.
Which browsers support MyDailyScribe?
Currently, MyDailyScribe is available for Chrome, with Firefox and Edge versions coming soon.
Does MyDailyScribe collect my data?
MyDailyScribe does not collect any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
